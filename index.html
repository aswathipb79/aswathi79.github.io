A few facts about the world we live in

Earth is our home planet, the third from the Sun, unique for being the only known place with life, thanks to its liquid water, protective atmosphere, and suitable temperature, featuring diverse ecosystems, tectonic plates, a core, mantle, and crust, and one moon, orbiting the Sun in about 365.25 days, creating seasons with its axial tilt.

  • Polar Diameter: 12,714 km
  • Mass:5.97 × 10^24 kg
  • Moons:1(The Moon)
